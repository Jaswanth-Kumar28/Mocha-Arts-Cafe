Our Story

Where Coffee Meets Fine Art

Mocha Art Cafe is more than a destination; it's a creative sanctuary. Born out of a deep passion for single-origin roasting and contemporary canvas creations, we offer a cozy refuge for coffee connoisseurs and art enthusiasts alike.

Every beverage we brew is prepared with precision from select high-altitude Arabica beans. Around you, the walls display work from talented local artists, creating an aesthetic atmosphere that inspires conversation and reflection.
